Weekly pick-up games in Helsinki

We play every week all around the year on Tuesdays around 6 pm. Place and exact time is confirmed at our Signal group. Contact Heikki (hw@iki.fi) if you wish to join.

No previous experience necessary – just show up if you are interested in trying the sport!

Competitions in 2025

  • 1st Feb Helsinki Winter Tournament (Monarch of the court format)
  • 27th April 2025 Helsinki Spring Tournament – registration open until 24th April
  • 9th–10th May 2025 Ilonranta Open (see iloranta.fi, contact sami.pihkala at elisanet.fi for information and registration)
  • 10th–15th August 2025 World Overall Flying Disc Championships
  • 23rd–24th August 2025 Finnish National Championship
  • 4th October 2025 Helsinki Open
  • November onwards: Helsinki Winter league

Tournament results are available at the global DDC website.
